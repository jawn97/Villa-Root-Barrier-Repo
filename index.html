Please let us introduce ourselves,

Welcome to Villa Root Barrier, the manufacturer of root diversion products and the distribution of tree planting & erosion control products for over 25 years. Our products are available throughout the United States with our full line-up of products available for immediate shipment.

Our Villa Root Barrier product line is manufactured to meet and exceed the industries standards. The line includes a true 2’ Villa Root Barrier Panel in standard depths of 1’, 18”, 24”, 36” and 48”. The Villa Root barrier panel can also be customized to greater depth to meet any special needs you may have. Our panel also offers an easy to install male / female connection on each panel. Our Villa Root Barrier roll is the most advanced root barrier in the industry. The newly redesigned Vacuum formed roll offers all the benefits of a panel in an easy to install roll. This roll has a 90 degree rib every 6 inches, ground stabilizers and an ease in install 20’ rolls. These rolls are presently manufactured in 12", 18”, 24" and 36” depths.
